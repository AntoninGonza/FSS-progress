Part 1 - Initial Project Proposal

Our Project is the FSS (finance society software), a specialized software designed exclusively for students at the American University of Paris (AUP). This innovative tool is here to simplify financial analysis and boost financial literacy among AUP students. With FSS, you gain access to a suite of features aimed at helping you accurately value firms. Whether you're working on a class project, internship, or personal research, our software offers various valuation methods like discounted cash flow, comparable company analysis, and precedent transactions analysis, along with easy data integration and real-time stock information. But that's not all - FSS also serves as a comprehensive financial dictionary, allowing you to quickly look up and understand complex financial terms and concepts. The user-friendly interface ensures that you can navigate and benefit from the software effortlessly, even if you're not a finance expert. You will also be able to see Finance Society news and events on the website. FSS is your all-in-one solution to excel in finance courses, make informed investment decisions, and build a strong foundation in financial knowledge. Start your journey towards financial expertise today with FSS.
